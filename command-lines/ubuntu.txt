to clear build storage (ubuntu server)

ssh alex@whatif-tool.ahdap.org
sudo su

** gives you a summary of disk space usage for each mounted filesystem on your system ** 
df -h

** see exactly whats inside the folder **
du -sh * | sort -hr

** will show you something like this **
2.6G	build
824M	files
70M	build_new

** check / path **
sudo du -hxd1 / | sort -hr | head -n 10

** usually it is the /var and /var/www that are big, clear em **
